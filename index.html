FAQ

Common questions, answered plainly

How long does business registration take?

Timelines depend on the Companies Registry's processing queue, but once your documents are ready we file promptly and keep you updated at every step.

What's the difference between sole trader and LLC registration?

A sole trader registration is simpler and cheaper, suited to individuals operating under a business name. An LLC creates a separate legal entity with liability protection, better suited to growing businesses and partnerships.

Do you work with non-profits and NGOs?

Yes. We support non-profits and community organisations with planning, branding and social media in addition to for-profit entrepreneurs and SMEs.

Can I order just one service, like a logo or business plan?

Absolutely. Every service can be booked individually, or combined into a bundle if you need several at once.
